Egg and Bacon Wrap

Ingredients

-1 onion, chopped

-1 tablespoon butter

-600ml thickened cream

-1/2 teaspoon salt

-1/4 teaspoon pepper

-2 cups shredded cheese

-1/2 pound sliced bacon, cooked and crumbled

-tortilla wraps

Steps

1. In frying pan, saute onions until translucent

2. In a bowl, beat the eggs, cream, salt and pepper

3. Pour over onions in frying pan

4. Sprinkle cheese and bacon to the frying pan

5. Cook and stir gently till eggs are set

6. Spoon the ingredients down the centre of the tortilla wrap and fold the sides over filling

7. Warm up in microwave for 1:30 minutes (if refrigerated)

8. Place in toaster till the wrap is completely toasted

Chicken and Avocado Focaccia

Ingredients

-focaccia bread

-1 poached chicken breast

-sliced colby cheese

-1/2 avocado

-loose spinach

-mayonaise

-salt

-pepper

Steps

1. Cut your focaccia bread in half horizontally

2. Add the spinach across the bottom

3. Cut the chicken and avocado into thin slices

4. Cut the cheese into triangles

5. Add chicken, avocado and cheese in that order

6. Heat focaccia in microwave for 30 seconds (if refrigerated)

7. Place focaccia into a toaster

8. Once the lid is toasted, take it out

9. Once the cheese melts, take the focaccia out

10. Spread mayonaise on the lid

11. Finally add salt and pepper

Hedgehog Slice

Ingredients

-2 packets of plain biscuits

-250g butter

-3 tablespoons cocoa powder

-3 tablespoons shredded coconut

-1 tin sweetened condensed milk

Steps

1. Crush bisuits in a bowl

2. Add cocoa powder and coconut and mix well

3. Melt butter and sweetened condesed milk together

4. Add to dry ingredients

5. Place into a slice pan and refrigerate

6. Wait till slice sets
